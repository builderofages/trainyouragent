# LinkedIn 30-Day Content Calendar — TrainYourAgent

**Goal:** Build founder authority + inbound flow. 1 post/day weekdays, 2 posts/weekend = 28 posts in 30 days.

**Format mix:** 60% text-only (highest reach 2025-2026), 25% screenshot + caption, 15% short Loom video.

**Hook formula:** First line is a contrarian claim, a specific number, or a story open. Second line creates a pattern interrupt. Third line is the body.

---

**Fri Day 5:** "I bought a Roomba on Amazon at 11pm last night. The 'we received your order' email landed 8 seconds later. Why does B2B SaaS still take 3 business days for an onboarding email? Solved this in 30 min with Resend + a 5-email drip."

**Sat Day 6:** Loom (90 sec) — "Watch me build a voice agent live in 3 minutes for a fake HVAC company." Screen-record. Show the prompts. End on "DM me if you want this for your real business."

**Sun Day 7:** "Week 1 of building TrainYourAgent in public. 2 leads, 0 customers. Brutal honesty thread..." → Reflect on the metrics dashboard. Show /admin/metrics screenshot. Build credibility through transparency.

## Engagement Daily Routine (30 min)

- 9:00 AM ET — post the day's content
- 9:05-9:25 — reply to every comment on yesterday's post
- 4:00 PM ET — reply to today's comments + DM the 3 strongest engagers
- Comment-on-others routine: 5 thoughtful comments/day on creator posts in your category (Mike Murchison, Owen Wilks, Sasha Orloff, Aaron Levie)

## What NOT to do

- No quote-tweet hot takes on Twitter drama
- No politically charged anything
- No "AI is gonna change everything" wishful-thinking posts — concrete only
- No sub-200-word posts unless it's a one-line zinger
- No more than 1 sales DM per inbound connection per week
- Never DM "thanks for connecting" — always lead with value (a relevant article, a specific question about their business)
