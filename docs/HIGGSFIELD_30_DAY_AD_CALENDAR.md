# 30-Day Higgsfield Ad Calendar — TrainYourAgent

Production-ready Marketing Studio prompts. One per day for 30 days.
Each prompt follows the [HIGGSFIELD_PROMPT_PLAYBOOK.md](./HIGGSFIELD_PROMPT_PLAYBOOK.md):
- `@image_1` = the pre-generated TrainYourAgent wordmark card (already in your
  GPT Image 2.0 library — drag it into the prompt area each day).
- Beat-by-beat timestamps, exact dialogue in quotes.
- Master negative-prompt boilerplate (hands, lip-sync, brand variants, clothing).
- STYLE LOCK so the founder face is consistent across the whole 30-day set.

**Daily ritual (~90 sec):** Open Marketing Studio → URL to Ad → attach
wordmark from Image Generations → paste day-N prompt → Generate. Done.

**Spend per day:** ~4,840 credits per ad. 30 days = ~145K credits. Plan a
top-up at the start of the month.

**Structure:**
- **Week 1** (Days 1-7) — Broad pain hooks. Maximize reach. Test which
  emotional hook lands.
- **Week 2** (Days 8-14) — HVAC niche deep-dive. Your highest-intent vertical.
- **Week 3** (Days 15-21) — Plumbing / Electrical / Roofing rotation.
- **Week 4** (Days 22-30) — Social proof, objection handling, founder story,
  direct CTAs.

  '[LINE 1]'
BEAT 5 to 7 seconds: lip-sync matches:
  '[LINE 2]'
BEAT 7 to 8 seconds: smooth fade to white, @image_1 brand wordmark holds.

CAMERA: single continuous handheld iPhone selfie shot, slight natural sway,
no whip-pans, no cuts. LIGHTING: [LIGHT NOTE]. SOUND DESIGN: [AMBIENT +

skin, no plastic skin, no beauty filter, no warping clothes, no melting

## How to run this for real

1. **Day 1 of the month:** generate Days 1-7 as a single batch in Marketing
   Studio. Higgsfield can run 4 jobs in parallel — kick all seven, walk away,
   come back in 20 min, download all seven.
2. **Same day:** upload all 7 to Meta Ads Manager as one ad set. Daily budget
   $50 minimum. Audience: 50-mile radius of Tampa Bay × Local Service
   Business owners × ages 28-58.
3. **Day 7:** read the metrics. The post-Andromeda framework: any ad with
   CTR < 1% and Hook Rate < 25% is dead. Pause it. Any ad with CTR > 1.5%
   is the winner — scale daily budget to $200.
4. **Days 8-14:** same drill with the HVAC niche batch, but target HVAC-owner
   look-alikes specifically.
5. **Days 15-21:** rotate niches. Run plumbing leads at plumbing
   look-alikes, electrical at electrical, etc.
6. **Days 22-30:** the closer batch. Retarget anyone who watched 75%+ of any
   Week-1-2-3 ad. These are the hottest leads — hit them with the founder
   story (Day 28) and the direct ask (Day 29).

**Expected total spend over 30 days:** $1,500-$3,000 on Higgsfield credits
plus $50-200/day Meta ad spend = $3,000-$9,000 / month. If just one HVAC
operator at $1,997/mo converts, you've paid this back in 90 days. Two
converts and you're cash positive on day 60.

---

## What you do NOT do

- Do not run all 30 ads at once. The framework requires staggered launch
  so Meta's algorithm can learn which hook your audience responds to.
- Do not edit the prompts without re-testing. The playbook structure is
  load-bearing — pulling out the negative-prompt boilerplate causes
  visible AI tells (warped hands, garbled brand text) within 8 seconds.
- Do not make a 31st ad before you have data on the first 30. The whole
  point is to find your winner, not to flood the feed.
- Do not run any of these on LinkedIn. LinkedIn ad costs are 5-8x Meta
  for B2B SMB and the audience overlap with operators is weak.

---

## When you find your winner

Take the winning prompt, batch it across 5-10 different avatars on the same
day (Higgsfield Marketing Studio supports avatar swap on a single project).
That's how you scale: one proven hook, ten faces, ten ads. Daily budget
follows performance.
