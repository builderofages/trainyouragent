## Top 3 conversion killers (verbatim)

1. **JS-heavy SPA that serves near-empty HTML to crawlers/bots/visitors on slow connections** — kills SEO, shareability, and first-impression speed for the exact busy trades owners you target.
2. **Vague broad positioning ("Everything-AI") + pricing complexity (5 lanes, mixed models)** = massive decision fatigue and "what the hell do I actually buy?" friction.
3. **Weak/sparse social proof** (thin /customers, pending testimonials, honest but not enough named HVAC wins front-and-center).

## Top 3 strengths (verbatim)

1. **Live interactive AI receptionist demos** (homepage + industry pages) — actual product proof that most competitors only promise.
2. **Brutally transparent pricing + ironclad guarantees** (30-day MBG, refunds if not shipped, no contracts, results-or-free language, ROI calculator) — rare credibility in the AI receptionist space.
3. **Founder-led, plain-English copy** with real Tampa HVAC operator audits and outcome focus — no marketing fluff, just numbers and "we ship or you don't pay."
